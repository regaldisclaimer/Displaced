Displaced.

Displaced is a free game available for Windows, Linux, and possibly many other platforms (Mac, Android, iOS, etc.).

You can play this game in your browser by [clicking here](#), or you can download the game on your computer, and play without installation!

Gameplay.

As the game starts, you discover yourself on an unknown distant planet, taking part in a competition to progress through stages as soon as possible

Controllers are supported, and recommended for smoother control of your character!
You can get one for a very low price ([like this](#)), and use it on your PC/Linux without any special setup!

As you make your way through the stages, you will find diverse and interesting challenges and obstacles.

Start Playing!

We don't want to give away too much spoilers, and would like to recommend you to just start playing the game on your browser here!.
